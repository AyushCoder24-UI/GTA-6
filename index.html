About GTA VI

The next chapter in the iconic Grand Theft Auto series takes you back to the neon-soaked streets of Vice City. Explore a vast, dynamic open world with unprecedented detail and immersion.

Featuring the series' first female protagonist, Lucia, alongside a returning character, experience a bold new direction in storytelling within the criminal underworld.

Jason is the male lead in GTA VI, a hardened yet ambitious criminal navigating Vice City’s ruthless underworld.He’s street-smart, resourceful, and driven by loyalty to those he trusts — especially Lucia.

Together, they balance brains and brawn: while Lucia strategizes, Jason executes with precision and fearlessness. His backstory hints at a troubled past and a desperate need to rise above it.
